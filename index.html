About Malaka Spice

Founded in 1997 by Praful and Cheeru Chandawarkar, Malaka Spice is a pioneer of Southeast Asian cuisine in India. What began as a small restaurant in Koregaon Park evolved into a culinary institution rooted in culture, travel, and storytelling.

Our Philosophy

Every Malaka Spice dish reflects journeys across Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Singapore, Vietnam, Cambodia, and Japan. We follow a farm-to-table approach with seasonal menus updated quarterly and responsibly sourced ingredients.
