About Malaka Spice

Founded in 1997 by Praful & Cheeru Chandawarkar, Malaka Spice is a celebrated Southeast Asian restaurant blending global flavors with innovation, community, and culture. Every dish tells a story of passion and exploration across Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore and beyond.

Our Story

Food at Malaka Spice is crafted from extensive culinary tours across Southeast Asia, bringing the finest ingredients, spices, and techniques to every plate.

Farm-to-table philosophy with seasonal menus refreshed quarterly.

Farm to Table

Malaka Spice sources fresh produce from Cherish Farms — a 4-acre initiative focused on sustainable, pesticide-free ingredients — enhancing both flavor and environmental impact.

Art & Ambience

Inside Malaka Spice, experience the Art Gallery showcasing rotating works from painters, sculptors, and mixed media creators.
